A little bit about me…

Hi, I'm Brandon! I'm old and I'm a web developer. My love for computers and technology in general started at an early age, and stemmed mostly from PC gaming. Before I graduated from high school, I built 3 desktop gaming PCs. I'd say that's really the time I started to intensely appreciate techonology. My affinity for technology and for development had grown exorbitantly by the time I graduated from Clemson University on May 11, 2018. I started programming on a whim ago. I really found that it's what I'm passionate about in that time. I often code in my free time, but when I'm not doing that or working in some other capacity you can catch me at the gym, on a hike, or out with friends.

After learning WordPress, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I got my first professional gig building a website for the Clemson Backpacking Club. The site was a great success for the club and formed the basis for sealing a website deal with the University Chair of Supply Chain & Logisitics, then one later with an associate professor of Engineering. All the while, I was still learning more about backend languages such as PHP, Python, and MySQL. Using these and my front end languages I built an array of personal projects, which are displayed alongside my professional projects below in my "Work" section.
